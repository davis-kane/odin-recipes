Roast Beef

Description

Here's a timeless roast beef recipe that can be made with rump roast, round roast, or sirloin roast. Slow-roasting on low heat works magic on tougher cuts of meat and provides a tender roast beef recipe you’ll love!

Ingredients

  • 3 to 3 1/2 pounds(1.3 to 1.6 kg) boneless rump roast (pick an end cut with a layer of fat, if you can) 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 8-10 slivers garlic (3 to 4 cloves, sliced in half or into thirds)
  • Salt and pepper

For Gravy

  • Red wine, water & beef stock
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch

Cooking Steps

  1. Salt the roast and let it come to room temp
  2. Preheat the oven to 375°F
  3. Insert slivers of garlic into the roast
  4. Rub the roast with olive oil, then season
  5. Put the roast on the rack with a pan below
  6. Brown at 375°F, then lower the heat to 225°F
  7. Roast to an internal temp of 135°F to 140°F
  8. Make the gravy as the roast rests
  9. Slice and serve
